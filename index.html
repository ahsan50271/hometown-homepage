Visit Torshov

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant
district close to the center of Oslo.

Top three activities to do at Torshov

Attend a show or a concert

The Torshov Theatre is a great
place to spend your night out.

Go for a picnic in a local park

The Torshov Park is perfect for picnic,
getting some tan or just chilling out.

Get sweaty in the sports field

Play football, volleyball,
basketball or lift some weights.
